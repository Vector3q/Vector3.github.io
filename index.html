Game: Read First. 2022

This is a 2D horizontal board action game developed by Unity ~ovo! Players take on the role of brave warriors tasked with eliminating unknown monsters. Before facing each boss, strategic decisions can be made on what skills to equip based on the provided information. Properly chosen skills can quickly defeat the boss, while inappropriate ones will great increase the difficulty of adventure.
