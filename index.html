Procedural Modeling of Trees. 2017

This is my implementation of the self-organizing algorithm for 3D tree modeling according to a SIGGRAPH paper [Palubicki et al.]. One can use it to automatically produce different types of trees by adjusting several procedural parameters.

[News] My code was also used by a recent paper in SIGGRAPH Asia. Please see their Acknowledgement section:
- TreePartNet: Neural Decomposition of Point Clouds for 3D Tree Reconstruction.
